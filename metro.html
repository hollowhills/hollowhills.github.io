Hollow Hills Metro

 

The metro is one of the fastest and most reliable ways to get around Hollow Hills.

 

Currently the metro features 8 lines.

 

Students learning in any Hollow Hills educational facility or college Ride on the Metro for FREE*.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

*Proper ID required. See the Hollow Hills Department of Education for more details

Why Metro

North / South Line

 

North and South line is one of the most used lines inside the Hollow Hills, connecting the North and the South of Hollow Hills.

 

It connects Destinations such as Pushnikov College of Modern Engineering and the Yelchin Memorial Movie Theatre.

North / South

East / West Line

 

East and West Line is similar to the North and West line. It also connects the East and The West line.

 

E&W line connects various neighborhoods in Hollow Hills with its gorgeous Downtown.

 

-:- Expect Delays -:-

East / West

Circle Line

 

Circle line was designed right underneath the Circle Avenue. The Line is designed to go both ways, contrary to the Avenue.

 

The circle line also intersects all the other lines, providing a great transfer line.

Circle Line

Airport Connect / Express

 

Airport Connect: A line designed to connect the Hollow Hills International Airport with Downtown with a few stops along the way.

 

Airport Express: A line designed to deliver passengers from Downtown Hollow Hills to the Airport without any stops.

 

 

 

 

 

Airport Connect / Airport Express

 

Outer Line

 

 

Hollow Hills Outer Line connects all of the destinations outside  Downtown Hollow Hills.

 

Including: The Airport, Suburban Neighborhoods, Hollow Hills Resort, Mayor's Mansion and the Grand Lake
